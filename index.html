Hello, My name is John Padron, better known as “Remer”. I was born and raised in San Antonio Texas, and although i bounced from many hobbies indecisively as a kid, there was not a moment in my life i didn’t have a hand in drawing.
As an artist, I think the need for supportive communities could be more emphasized. The need for informative, encouraging and guiding voices around you helps you stay motivated, eager, and attentive to wanting to progress and thrive in pursuing your goals.
